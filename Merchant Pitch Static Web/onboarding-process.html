Step-by-Step Onboarding Process

Follow these simple steps to get your business onboarded and start growing with Winlucks:

1

Application

Download the Winlucks Merchant app or apply through our website. Fill in your basic business information to get started.

  • Provide business name and contact information
  • Select your business category
  • Choose your preferred plan (Subscription or Postpaid)
2

Document Submission

Submit the required documents for verification. Our team will review them promptly.

  • Trade license copy
  • Emirates ID of owner/manager
  • Bank account details for settlements
  • Store photos (optional but recommended)
3

Verification

Our team verifies your documents and business information. This process is usually completed within 4-8 hours.

  • Document authenticity check
  • Business information validation
  • Quick approval process
4

Store Setup

Set up your digital storefront on Winlucks with your products, prices, and business information.

  • Add your product catalog with images
  • Set your pricing and inventory
  • Configure business hours and location
  • Set up delivery/pickup options
5

Rewards Configuration

Set up your reward coins system to attract and retain customers.

  • Decide on coin allocation per purchase
  • Create special offers and promotions
  • Set up loyalty programs for repeat customers
6

Go Live & Start Selling

Once everything is set up, your store goes live on Winlucks and you can start receiving orders immediately.

  • Receive real-time order notifications
  • Manage orders through merchant dashboard
  • Track sales and customer analytics
  • Get paid on regular settlement cycles

Document Requirements

To ensure a smooth onboarding process, please have these documents ready:

Business Documents

  • Trade license copy
  • Chamber of commerce certificate
  • VAT certificate (if applicable)

Owner/Manager Documents

  • Emirates ID (front and back)
  • Passport copy
  • Visa page (for expatriates)

Banking Information

  • Bank account details
  • Bank statement (last 3 months)
  • Cancelled cheque (if available)
