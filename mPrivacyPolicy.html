1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Winlucks LLC-FZ ("Winlucks," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and safeguards the personal and business information of merchants ("you" or "Merchant") while using the Winlucks Merchant App (the "Platform"). By accessing or using the Platform, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Winlucks is committed to compliance with applicable data protection laws in the UAE.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Provided by You

  • Account Registration: Business name, license details, contact information, and Know Your Customer (KYC) documents.
  • Transaction Information: Details related to sales, payouts, and refunds.
  • Support Requests: Any information provided when contacting customer support.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system, and browser type.
  • Platform Activity: Usage patterns, login history, and pages visited.

2.3 Third-Party Information

  • Payment processors may share transaction details for order validation and payouts.
  • Analytics providers may share anonymized usage data to help improve Platform functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Operational Use

  • To create and manage your merchant account.
  • To process transactions, deduct commissions, and issue payouts.

3.2 Communication

  • To notify you of changes to Platform policies, terms, or features.
  • To provide updates about Platform performance and promotional opportunities.

3.3 Compliance and Security

  • To comply with legal obligations, such as financial reporting and tax compliance.
  • To detect, prevent, and mitigate fraudulent activities.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist in payment processing, cloud storage, and analytics.

4.2 Customers

Limited information, such as your business name, contact details, and product offerings, may be displayed to customers using the Platform.

4.3 Legal Obligations

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or other legal processes.

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of the transaction.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or to comply with legal, regulatory, and tax obligations. Upon account termination, data will be securely deleted or anonymized unless retention is required by law.

6. Data Security

Winlucks implements security measures, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive information like passwords.
  • Firewalls and secure servers.
  • Regular audits and system updates.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data.
  • Correction: Request updates to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request data deletion, subject to legal obligations.
  • Restriction: Limit how your data is processed.
  • Portability: Obtain your data in a portable format.

For assistance, contact privacy@winlucks.com.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Platform uses cookies to enhance your experience and analyse usage. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email or in-app notifications.

10. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the United Arab Emirates. Disputes will be resolved through arbitration under the rules of the Dubai International Arbitration Centre (DIAC).

11. Contact Information

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@winlucks.com.
