Merchant Benefits & Features

Winlucks offers a comprehensive set of features designed to help your business grow and succeed.

  • Free Basic Plan - Includes rewards, product management, and promotions. Paid plans offer additional e-commerce features.
  • Product Listing - Upload product details, pricing, and availability via the vendor dashboard.
  • Customizable Deals & Rewards - Create exclusive deals, discounts, and rewards to attract customers.
  • Reward Coins & Incentive System - Customers earn coins with purchases; use coins for additional discounts and prizes.
  • Vendor Dashboard & Order Management - Manage orders and track sales performance through an intuitive dashboard.
  • Instant Alerts & Exclusive Savings - Receive notifications for rewards and special offers to plan shopping effectively.
  • Exposure to Hidden Shops & Products - Winlucks helps customers discover lesser-known shops and products.
  • Coin Gifting - Send reward coins as gifts to customers, adding a personal touch to promotions.
