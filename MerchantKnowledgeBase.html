Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about using Winlucks:

How do I set up reward coins for my customers?

You can set up reward coins through your merchant dashboard. Go to the "Rewards" section, click "Create Reward Program," and choose the percentage of purchase value you want to allocate as coins. You can also set up special bonus coin events for specific products or during promotions.

How often will I receive payments from Winlucks?

Winlucks processes payments weekly. You'll receive your settlement every Tuesday for all orders completed in the previous week (Monday-Sunday). The funds are typically deposited directly to your bank account within 1-2 business days after processing.

Can I integrate Winlucks with my existing POS system?

Yes, Winlucks offers API integration for popular POS systems. Contact our merchant support team to discuss integration options. We also provide manual import/export options for inventory and order management if full integration isn't available for your specific system.

How can I optimize my product listings for better visibility?

To optimize your product listings: use high-quality images from multiple angles, write detailed descriptions with relevant keywords, set competitive prices, use all available product attributes, and encourage customer reviews. Products with complete information and positive reviews rank higher in search results.

What should I do if I encounter technical issues with the merchant app?

First, try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, check for app updates in your app store. For ongoing issues, contact our technical support team at support@winlucks.com or call +971 58 98 50 10 2. Please describe the issue and include screenshots if possible for faster resolution.
